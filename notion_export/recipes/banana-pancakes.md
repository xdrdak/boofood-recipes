# Banana Pancakes

Tags: Breakfast, Dairy
Contributor: Gwyneth Pang
Created On: May 11, 2022 10:50 AM
Reference: https://www.theconsciousplantkitchen.com/baby-banana-pancakes/
Ingredients: Banana, Flour, Milk
Number of Portions: 2

# Ingredients (makes 4)

- [ ]  1 whole Banana (ripe)
- [ ]  5 Tbsp Whole Wheat Flour (or 3 heaping giant soup spoons piled to the brim)
- [ ]  2 Tbsp Milk
- [ ]  Salt to taste

# Instructions

1. In a bowl, mash banana with a fork
2. Add flour and mix until all flour has been hydrated
3. Add milk and mix
    1. The texture should resemble very thick goop, similar to jam
4. Heat non stick pan and cook pancakes

# Notes

Whole wheat flour tastes better in this recipe than normal AP flour, but it will still work nontheless
