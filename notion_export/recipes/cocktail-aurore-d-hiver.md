Tags: Drinks
Contributor: Gwyneth Pang
Created On: December 3, 2023 3:29 PM

- [ ]  1 oz Gin
- [ ]  1/2 oz Aperol (or orange électrique)
- [ ]  2 oz Mandarine Juice (or Orange juice)
