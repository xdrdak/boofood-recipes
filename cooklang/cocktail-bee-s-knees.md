[- RECIPE_START -]
-- Recipe for Bee's Knees

In a shaker, combine:
@gin{2 oz},
@lemon juice{3/4 oz},
@honey syrup{3/4 oz} (2:1 ratio of honey to water).

Shake well and serve in a martini glass.

Garnish with a lemon twist.

[- RECIPE_END -]
