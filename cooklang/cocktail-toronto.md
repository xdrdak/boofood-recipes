-- Recipe for Toronto Cocktail

In a mixing glass, combine:
@rye whiskey{2 oz},
@fernet{3/4 oz},
@maple syrup{1-2 barspoons},
@angostura bitters{2 dashes or 1 barspoon}.

Stir well with ice and serve straight up.
