[- RECIPE_START -]
-- Recipe for Vodka Cranberry Soda

In a shaker, combine:
@vodka{1 oz},
@cranberry juice{2 oz}.

Shake well without soda.

Fill the glass with soda and a dash of cinnamon.

[- RECIPE_END -]
