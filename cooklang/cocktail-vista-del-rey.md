[- RECIPE_START -]
-- Recipe for Vista del Rey

In a shaker, combine:
@melon liqueur{3/4 oz},
@tequila{1 oz},
@cream of mint{1/4 oz},
@lime juice{1/2 oz},
@szechuan peppercorn{1/4 tsp, crushed}.

Shake well and serve with ice.
